# The Impact of Social Media on Mental Health

## Introduction

Social media has become an integral part of our daily lives, but its impact on mental health is a topic of growing concern. Let's explore the positive and negative effects of social media usage.

## Positive Impacts

1. Increased connectivity
2. Access to information
3. Community building
4. Platform for self-expression

## Negative Impacts

1. <Define definition="A fear of missing out on rewarding experiences that others might be having">FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)</Define>
2. Comparison and low self-esteem
3. Cyberbullying
4. Addiction and decreased productivity

<Warning>
  Excessive use of social media can lead to increased feelings of anxiety, depression, and loneliness.
</Warning>

## Balancing Act

To maintain a healthy relationship with social media:

- Set time limits for usage
- Curate your feed to include positive content
- Practice mindfulness and self-awareness
- Engage in real-world social interactions
